Helping others with your computer

A computer is a vital tool for anyone living in this day and age. This is why we aim to donate every 1 in 10 computers we collect to charitabe and worthy causes.


Safe & Secure

Eco-Friendly

Helping Others

We donate 1 in 10 the machnes to charities and worthy causes. This helps grow individuals who are in need of the benefits a computer can bring.

Welcome to We Collect Your IT

We offer a fee collection service across the UK for the removal of your old IT systems. We safely reuse this equipment by enseuring any sensitie or personal information that was on these machines is either physically removed or destroyed before we reuse them. Using us shall not only benefit the environment by avoiding the waste and disposal if this equipment but shall also help othres, as we donate and proportion of all the equipment we collect.
